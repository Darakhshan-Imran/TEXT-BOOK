### SR3: Key Topics Overview (150-200 words)
**Hardware Selection**: A physical AI lab requires careful selection of robots, sensors, computing platforms, and development tools. This includes choosing between different robot platforms (wheeled, legged, manipulator arms), sensors (cameras, LIDAR, IMUs), and computing systems based on project requirements and budget constraints.

**Safety Protocols**: Working with physical robots requires comprehensive safety measures including physical barriers, emergency stops, collision detection, and proper ventilation. These protocols protect both researchers and equipment during development and testing phases.

**Lab Organization**: An efficient lab layout maximizes productivity with dedicated spaces for development, testing, and storage. This includes areas for electronics work, mechanical assembly, and safe robot operation with appropriate flooring, lighting, and power distribution.
