### SR3: Key Topics Overview (150-200 words)
**Sensory Fusion**: Multi-modal interaction combines visual, auditory, tactile, and other sensory inputs to create a comprehensive understanding of the environment. This fusion enables robots to operate effectively in complex, dynamic environments where single modalities may be insufficient.

**Cross-Modal Reasoning**: Modern AI systems can reason across different modalities, understanding relationships between visual scenes, spoken language, and physical interactions. This enables robots to follow instructions that reference visual elements or explain their actions using natural language.

**Human-Robot Interfaces**: Multi-modal interaction enables more natural human-robot interfaces that combine speech, gesture, visual attention, and physical interaction. This creates more intuitive and effective collaboration between humans and robots.
