### SR3: Key Topics Overview (150-200 words)
**Language Model Integration**: Large language models (LLMs) like GPT, Claude, and others can serve as high-level cognitive controllers for robots, interpreting natural language commands and generating appropriate action sequences. This integration requires specialized interfaces to connect language understanding with physical action execution.

**Planning and Reasoning**: LLMs excel at high-level reasoning and planning, breaking down complex tasks into executable steps. When connected to robotic systems, they can generate detailed action plans based on natural language instructions and environmental context.

**Embodied Cognition**: Connecting LLMs to physical robots creates embodied AI systems that can understand spatial relationships, manipulate objects, and navigate environments guided by language-based reasoning, bridging the gap between symbolic and physical intelligence.
